Feature Analysis
What drives
property value?
📐
Area is the dominant signal
Floor area accounts for 38% of predictive power. Each 100 sq ft adds ~$9,500 at suburban baseline.
📍
Location multiplies everything
Prime metro commands a 1.45× premium — a 45% uplift on every other feature purely from location.
🕐
Age decay is capped
Properties lose 1.2% value per year, but depreciation is capped at 25%. A 40-year home still retains 75% value.
🚗
Garage adds flat $12,000
Before location multipliers — meaning a garage in Prime metro is effectively worth $17,400.
